About Me

Hello, I'm Grace!

I'm a passionate travel and photography enthusiast who loves capturing moments from around the world. Photography has always been a way for me to tell stories — from quiet city streets to breathtaking landscapes.

This website is a collection of my favorite photographs from my travels. Through my lens, I hope to share not only beautiful views but also the emotions and memories that come with them.

My Photography Journey

I first picked up a camera when I started traveling during college, and it quickly became my favorite companion. I'm drawn to natural lighting, candid moments, and exploring the connection between people and places.
